With blurbs, you can add your favorite bookmarks to our site so that you'll never lose another bookmark again. Whether you're upgrading to a new device, or refreshing and old one, you can find your bookmarks on Blurbs.

When you use Blurbs, you'll be able to add categories and organize your bookmarks by type. Over time, you'll be able to see your collection of links change and grow.
